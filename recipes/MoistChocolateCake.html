Moist Chocolate Cake

Description

Today it is how to make chocolate mousse cake recipe, This Chocolate Cake recipe is a classic!
With layer of moist chocolate cake and layer of smooth and creamy chocolate mousse, all covered
in dark chocolate. This mousse cake recipe so delicious & satisfying, you will enjoy every bites of it.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • flour (or maida) 1 & 1/2 cup
  • cocoa powder 4 tbsp
  • baking powder 1 tsp
  • baking soda 1/2 tsp
  • sugar 1/2 cup
  • cooking oil 1/2 cup
  • instant coffee 1 tbsp
  • warm water 350ml
  • white vinegar or lemon juice 1 tbsp

For the mousse:

  • whipping cream 400ml
  • powdered sugar 3/4 cup
  • cocoa powder 1/4 cup

For the chocolate ganache:

  • semi sweet dark chocolate 200g
  • milk 1/2 cup
  • some grated chocolate on top

Steps

  1. Mix in a plate all dry ingredints; flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda
    and the sugar

  2. Then add cooking oil, instant coffee, warm water and the lemon juice to the dry
    (mixed earlier). Mix it well combined then bake in a baking tin of 6inch.

  3. Place a stand in a pot. Cover and heat the pot for 5-10 mins. Place the cake tin
    inside the pot. Bake pre-heated oven at 170C for 45-50 mins. Let it cool.

  4. In an other plate beat the whipping cream for 1 min then add powdered sugar, cocoa
    powder and beat all until stiff peaks

  5. Cut the baked cake in two parts. Place the first layer in the Cake ring ogf
    6inch, pour the whipped cream and place the second layer. Keep aside

  6. Mix the dark chocolate and the mild and melt it in the microwave for 1 min.
    Mix it well.

  7. Pour the melted chocolate over the second layer and spread evenly. Keep in
    the refrigerator for 2-4 hours or until set. Some grated chocolate (optional).
